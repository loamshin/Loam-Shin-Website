Majestic Deck

A prototype that allows you to play with a deck of cards.

Design Process

Duration: 2 months

As I listed the primary actions involved in the card app, my utmost priority was to create a layout that felt effortlessly natural and intuitive for users. A crucial aspect of this endeavor was the deliberate choice of a user-friendly and minimalistic card design, ensuring an appealing visual hierarchy that enhances the overall user experience.

      

Final Prototype

My takeaway

I loved working on this project because it gave me the chance to extensively use Figma. I discovered many new features that I hadn't known before, and they made my work much more efficient. Although it was challenging initially, I found it easier with practice. Utilizing components and variants saved me time, and the iterative process improved my design skills and Figma proficiency. Seeing the final result was incredibly rewarding!
